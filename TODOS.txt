1. Localisation:
set up languages using top level route segment:
/en/app1/...
/en/app2/...
/de/app1/...
/de/app2/...


Why this is the best approach

•  SEO‑friendly
•  Cache‑friendly
•  Works with CDNs
•  MFEs stay independent
•  No global state needed
•  No query‑string pollution
